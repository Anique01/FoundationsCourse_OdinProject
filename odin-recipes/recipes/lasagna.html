Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe brings together layers of rich, savory marinara sauce, perfectly seasoned ground beef, and a blend of creamy ricotta and mozzarella cheeses. The combination of these ingredients creates a comforting dish that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions. The top layer of melted cheese gives it a golden, bubbly finish that makes it irresistible.

For added depth of flavor, this recipe allows for customization with additional ingredients like fresh basil or spinach. The layers are baked to perfection, ensuring each bite is a satisfying mix of tender noodles, hearty meat, and creamy sauce. Whether you’re an experienced cook or a beginner, this lasagna recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes (or 700g tomato passata)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • A pinch of sugar (optional)

Béchamel Sauce

  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml whole milk
  • salt, to taste
  • A pinch of nutmeg (optional)

Other ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets (fresh or dried)
  • 250g ricottta cheese
  • 200g mozzarella, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions

Prepare the meat sauce:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it apart with a spoon until browned.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, oregano, basil, and canned tomatoes (or passata). If using canned tomatoes, crush them lightly with a spoon.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar (to balance the acidity of the tomatoes).
  5. Simmer the sauce on low heat for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

Make the béchamel sauce:

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and stir for about 1 minute to make a roux.
  2. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking continuously to prevent lumps.
  3. Continue cooking and stirring until the sauce thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and add a pinch of nutmeg if desired. Remove from heat.

Assemble the lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. If using dried lasagna sheets, cook them according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  4. Place a layer of lasagna sheets over the sauce.
  5. Add another layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce, then a few spoonfuls of ricotta.
  6. Repeat the layers (meat sauce, béchamel, ricotta) until you have 3-4 layers, finishing with a layer of béchamel sauce.
  7. Top with grated mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

Bake:

  1. Cover the lasagna with foil (to prevent it from drying out) and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbly.
  3. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
